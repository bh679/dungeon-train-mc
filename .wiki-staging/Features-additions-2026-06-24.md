## Advancements — New (June 2026)

**Hell on Rails** *(v0.357.0)*
Earned by riding the train deep into the Nether band (triggers well inside the netherrack core, not at the first edge). Sits just before *Voided Warranty* in the advancement tree.

---

## Difficulty Scaling — Item Stats *(v0.358.0)*

Weapons and armour now scale with the carriage's difficulty tier:
- +0.10 attack damage / armour per difficulty level
- +0.15 per level for axes
- Applies to chest, container, and vase loot; also to PlayerMob equipment
- Secondary stats (attack speed, toughness) and regular mob gear unchanged

---

## Books — Quiet Rules Addition *(v0.355.0)*

The *Quiet Rules of the Train* book gained a new rule: worn-down weapons and armour can be mended by combining two of the same item. The left-hand item's stats are preserved in the result.
