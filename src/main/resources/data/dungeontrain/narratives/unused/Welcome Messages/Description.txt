Letter 01-
Dungeon Train is an infinite train of procedural generated dungeon carriages.

Mindless fun. Explore endless and varying carriages, items, equipment, magic, leveling, enemies and terrain. Lose yourself in a beautiful  world of dungeon crawling and looting, and forget about everything.

Developed by an award winning VR developer.  Each run takes your further into the train, further into the universe. A game designed outside laws of physics,  to go forever.

Every train cart is a unique adventure, a new space to understand, new items to explore, new terrain to absorb.

Every run is a new story, learn the lore of the train by discovering the journeys of countless past explorers and passengers.

Every story adds to your personal narrative. Forget about the real world, forget your life outside this train.

Just enjoy the illusive endlessness this game creates. Don't question it. Why risk your mindless fun for the sake of trying to read further into it? What good can come from questions?
